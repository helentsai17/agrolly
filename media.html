Agrolly follows up with the farmers in the eastern provinces, Khentii

Agrolly’s representative in Mongolia visits Khentii, one of the eastern provinces, to explain more about Agrolly in detail and teach how farmers can use the app in the long run. Mongolia has extreme weather, it is very cold in winter, hot in summer, chilly in both autumn and spring. The farmers will succeed for production if they effectively fight against climate change. Agrolly can make it possible for farmers to tackle the challenges of climate change and take preventive action against the possible risks.

Agrolly introduced to the western province of Mongolia, Bayankhongor

Mongolia representative visited Bayankhongor, the western province of Mongolia, and introduced Agrolly to the farmers and agronomists. The farmers have highlighted the weather and crop plantation functions of Agrolly since the biggest challenge for them is knowing about what is going to happen to weather one year ahead and then make efficient prevention and plan efficient crop production. Agrolly is a main tool to fight against climate change. Agronomists say that Agrolly is an innovative solution in agricultural farming in Mongolia.
